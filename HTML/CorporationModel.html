The Corporation Model

Another useful model is the Corporation model. Just as the Diplomacy model built on the Free-For-All model, the Corporation Model builds on the Diplomacy model! It does this by creating layers of Diplomacy models in a tree-like fashion, where each group of diplomats is considered a represented group for another group of diplomats. This creates a hierarchical structure where there is a top-most level of diplomats (think of a group composed of the CEO, CFO, etc... of an actual corporation) and each diplomat has a subgroup that they communicate with.   • group dynamics within a bureaucracy,
  • and any problem where there is a defined hierarchy where each level in the hierarchy only communicates with the ones above and below.
