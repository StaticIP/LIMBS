HOTCO Agents

Now that you’ve got a handle on the basic features of the LIMBS framework, you can start delving into the agents in the simulation. HOTCO, or hot coherence, is a cognitive model that incorporates emotion and belief based on cognitive effective maps, small semantic networks that link concepts by how coherent or incoherent they are with each other.

HOTCO provides you with a basis for customizing each individual agent to act as exactly that, individuals!
