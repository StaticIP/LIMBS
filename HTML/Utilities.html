Utilities

Utilities are like Agents in many ways. They communicate, they process messages, they have the same grouping principles. The one thing that makes Utilities different is their lightweighted-ness. Utilities lack emotional processing power and emotional communication, making them perfect to represent large entities or facilities that don't engage in emotional thought. Facilities like an elections committee, or the random fluctuations in a stock market.

There are three utilities currently in LIMBS:
  • Logging Utilities help you analyze transient behaviour in the simulation by outputing messages to a file
  • Event Utilities help you schedule external events which are not attributable to Agents, such as the weather, birthdays, or mudslides.
  • Polling Utilities helop you run referendums and collect aggregate information from multiple Agents.
